1️⃣ Project Idea
Data Source: The project is based on analyzing a supermarket dataset obtained from Kaggle.

Main Objective: To carefully study and analyze this data to extract clear insights and performance indicators that help the supermarket management or owner make smart business decisions.

Project Value:

Developing product quality and increasing sales volume and profits.

Optimizing store investments and cutting down wasted expenses in the wrong areas.

Transforming complex data and huge tables into an interactive dashboard that is very simple, clear, and easy to understand.

Final Outcome: Enabling management to clearly see the strengths and weaknesses of their business—which were hard to notice without analysis—to provide real solutions that improve operational efficiency and boost the store's economy.

The workflow of the project included:

Importing the dataset into Power BI

Building the complete data model

Creating all required calculated columns (fields) and business measures

Applying a wide range of visualizations

Designing a visually appealing and user-friendly dashboard

Special attention was given to:

Dashboard layout

Color selection

Visual consistency

The goal was to deliver a dashboard that is not only analytical, but also engaging and easy to explore for any user.
